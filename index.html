Re-create characters from all your favorite classic video games!

Users can add rows when they click that "Add Row" button. Clicking on a cell will toggle certain color.Whatever color has been chosen by the user, and then change the cells that we click to be that color. It can be tiresome to have to click each cell individually. So user's can that dragging the mouse over cells will "paint" them the specified color! You'll take advantage of "mouseup", "mousedown" that you used to drag and drop cats, as well as the "mouseover".
